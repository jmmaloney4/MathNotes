\section{The Complexity of Sets}

\subsection{Computably Enumerable Sets}

\begin{align}
	W_e 
	= \text{dom} \Parens{\Pe} 
	= \Set{ i \in \N | \Cvgs{\PeP{i}} }
\end{align}

Then \( \Parens{W_e}_{e \in \N} \) is an effective listing of all c.e. sets.
